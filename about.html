Independent Technical Support for Home Users

Who We Are

Printer PC Support is an independent technical support provider helping users with common printer, computer, email and internet issues.

Our goal is to provide friendly, professional and easy-to-understand assistance for everyday technology problems.

We support a wide range of devices and software including printers, Windows computers, laptops, email applications and home Wi-Fi networks.
