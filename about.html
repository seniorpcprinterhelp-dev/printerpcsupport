About Printer PC Support

Printer PC Support is an independent technical support provider helping home users with common printer, computer, email and internet issues.

Our goal is to provide simple, friendly and professional assistance for everyday technology problems.

We support a wide range of devices and software including printers, Windows computers, laptops, email applications and home Wi-Fi networks.
