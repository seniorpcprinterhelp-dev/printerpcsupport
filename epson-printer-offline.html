How to Fix Epson Printer Offline

Simple Troubleshooting Guide

Why Is My Epson Printer Offline?

Your Epson printer may appear offline due to Wi-Fi issues, printer settings, driver problems or communication errors.

Step 1

Check that the printer is powered on.

Step 2

Make sure the printer and computer are connected to the same Wi-Fi network.

Step 3

Restart your printer, router and computer.

Step 4

Set Epson as your default printer in Windows settings.
