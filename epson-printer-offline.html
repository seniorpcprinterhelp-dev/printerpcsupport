How to Fix Epson Printer Offline

If your Epson printer appears offline, try these troubleshooting steps.

Step 1: Check Printer Connection

Ensure the printer is powered on and connected to Wi-Fi or USB.

Step 2: Restart Devices

Restart your printer, computer, and router.

Step 3: Set Printer as Default

Open printer settings and set your Epson printer as the default printer.

Step 4: Update Drivers

Install the latest printer drivers from the manufacturer website.
