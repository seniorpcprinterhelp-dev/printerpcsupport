Canon Printer Not Printing

Easy Troubleshooting Guide

Why Is My Canon Printer Not Printing?

Paper jams, low ink, connection issues or incorrect settings can stop a Canon printer from printing.

Step 1

Check paper and ink levels.

Step 2

Restart the printer and computer.

Step 3

Make sure Canon is selected as the default printer.

Step 4

Run the Windows printer troubleshooter.
